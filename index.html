About us

Innovative Quality Solutions and Services (IQSS) is a minority owned company who started operations back 1998 in Chihuahua City as "Communications Solutions and Services" (CSS), and have grown from security and communications equipment retail sales to mobile phones and hotel services in 2004 to Solutions and Services Supplier in 2009.

Our company is competitive offering third party sort and re-work that can be completed either at your facility or at ours. We as well provide a wide range of on-site management Solutions and Services for Suppliers and Manufacturers.

We provides liaison services to Suppliers and Manufacturers. Acting as a two-way conduit between supplier and customer, we serve the needs of both to ensure a continuing and solid relationship.

Our company and warehouse is conveniently located near to Ysleta (Zaragoza) International Border at El Paso and Near to Parque Central at Juarez Mexico for easy pick-up and delivery. Our warehouse provides a secure storage area with over 5,000 sq. ft. of space.

IQSS Corp. provides Engineers that are experienced in the fields of Electrical, Computer, Mechanical, Quality Control and Industrial Engineering. Our engineers are trained in accordance with ISO-TS 16949 and ISO 9001:2008 standards. Our engineering services varies from implementing ISO standards to the development of work instructions, pictorial aides, control plans, PPAP’s, FMEA’s, process auditing, corrective and preventive actions, as well as error proofing and APQP timelines.
